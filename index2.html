Thanksgiving Dessert Game

For Thanksgiving Desserts customers want to place orders for the total number of guests they will be serving. This is the number displayed on the left. When you click on one of the 4 desserts below, it will add a set number of servings to your total number of dessert servings. If your score matches the Requested number of servings, you win. If you go over the amount of servings of dessert requested, you lose.
